# Support ZotAssets / 支持 ZotAssets

ZotAssets is free and open source. The most helpful support is simple: star the repository, report clear issues, and share the plugin with other Zotero users who need better attachment organization.

ZotAssets 是免费开源项目。最直接的支持是给仓库点 Star、提交清晰的问题反馈，并把插件分享给需要整理 Zotero 附件的朋友。
